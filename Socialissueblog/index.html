SOCIAL ISSUES


What are Social Issues?

A social issue is a problem that influences many citizens within a society. It is a common problem in present-day society and one that many people strive to solve. It is often the consequence of factors extending beyond an individual's control. Social issues are the source of a conflicting opinion on the grounds of what is perceived as morally correct or incorrect personal life or interpersonal social life decisions. In simple words,it means a social problem is any condition or behavior that has negative consequences for large numbers of people.
